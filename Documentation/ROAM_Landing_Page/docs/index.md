## Overview
The ROAM / Rapid Prototyping Lab (RPL) Robotic Welding Senior Design Project (Fall 2025 – Spring 2026) is building a practical robotic welding cell around the UFactory xArm 850.

Our team plan centers on delivering safe, repeatable weld execution with process logging, while maintaining a strong low-cost design objective. We are targeting an overall system objective near **$20,000**, approximately **1/4 the cost** of many higher-priced robotic welding solutions.

## Team Plan
1. **Mechanical + electrical integration:** finalize torch mounting and cell layout.
2. **Safety-first commissioning:** validate E-stop behavior and safety boundaries.
3. **Controls bring-up:** run repeatable weld paths through Python/ROS workflows.
4. **Data capture + iteration:** log process parameters and refine performance over successive tests.

## System Snapshot
- **Robot:** UFactory xArm 850  
- **Welder:** Miller MillerMatic Pro  
- **End Effector:** Custom torch mount and adapter  
- **Safety:** Welding light curtains and emergency stops  
- **Control Stack:** UFactory Studio, Python, ROS
